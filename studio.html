07 / Process

What working with us looks like.

  1. 01

    We talk for thirty minutes.

    You explain the problem. We tell you whether we can help. If we can't, we say who can.

  2. 02

    We send a one-page brief.

    Scope, deliverables, price, timeline. Either you sign it or you don't. We don't do follow-ups.

  3. 03

    We ship every Friday.

    You see what's built each week. You can stop at the end of any week with no penalty.

  4. 04

    We hand it over and step back.

    Code, brand, content, accounts. All yours. We answer questions for thirty days.

What we don't do.

We don't pitch.

We don't subcontract.

We don't take a brief we can't make great.

Ready when you are.

Tell us what you're trying to ship. We reply within two working days, or tell you it's not a fit.
